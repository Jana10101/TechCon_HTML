History

TechCon began in 2010 as a local meetup for Silicon Valley developers. Over the past decade, it has evolved into a global technology conference, attracting innovators, researchers, and industry leaders from around the world. Each year, attendees gather to share breakthroughs in AI, cloud computing, cybersecurity, and more.

Mission

Our mission is to foster collaboration and spark innovation across all areas of technology. TechCon is committed to providing an inclusive platform where developers, engineers, entrepreneurs, and students can learn about emerging trends, share best practices, and build relationships that drive the future of the industry.
